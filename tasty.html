01, Project Overview
The product:

Tasty app is a free cookbook app with over 4000 Tasty recipes. Provide super-short cooking tutorials and Step-By-Step instruction mode; a ‘My Recipes’ page which serves as your very own mobile cookbook, and an incredible Search tool that allows you to filter by any ingredients, cuisine, and social occasion.

Project Goal:

This project aims to redesign Tasty app.By explores target users cooking habits and challenges they face while using cooking recipe’s app to uncovers potential areas for design intervention.

My role:

UX Research, UI Designer, Branding

05, Competitive Analysis

I analysed Tasty’s top competitors to evaluate their strengths and weaknesses. This information helped me to understand what the trends are across all competitors, their unique differences, and help me to identify the possible opportunities for our product.

07, User Research

To learn about user personal experiences with recipe’s app and Tasty, I conducted user interviews. I interviewed 5 people between the ages of 25-50, 10-15 minutes each, asking open-ended questions to learn about their experiences with cooking recipes digital product , key insights were uncovered:

08, Defining the Problems

I use the insights and needs gained from research to identify what the main problems are that I am trying to solve. I used those insights and needs to create POV statements to better understand the problem from the user’s perspective and then created HMW questions to come up with possible solutions for these problems.

POV

01

Natalie needs to be able to use the recipes app without needing to touch the screen because she uses it while cooking.

02

Natalie needs to be able to find accurate recommendations because she wants to discover recipes that fit her diet preferences.

03

Natalie needs to be able to track their meal nutrition information because they concern and care for their eating habit

HMW

01

How might we help Natalie experience the app while cooking without touching the screen?

02

How might we help Natalie discovers new recipes that she likes through recommendations?

03

How might we help Natalie to track her meal nutrition information?

10, Visual Identity

Rebranding the Tasty

The brand's essence is “trusted friend”. Tasty's key brand associations are a wholesome welcoming  product, easy to use, tasty food. Based on my research findings, users care about healthy diet lifestyle, the rebranding version of tasty should reinforces the idea of caring and well-being. The interface has a minimal and calm approach, using pale blue tone colours match with bright highlight colours. This aesthetic aids in inducing balance and ease in users.
